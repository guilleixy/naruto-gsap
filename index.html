Naruto
Naruto Uzumaki is a fictional character from the Japanese manga and anime series, "Naruto." He is the titular protagonist and a ninja with dreams of becoming the strongest ninja, the Hokage, and earning the respect of his village, Konohagakure. Naruto's journey is characterized by his indomitable spirit, determination, and the pursuit of acceptance.

Sasuke
Sasuke Uchiha is a fictional character from the Japanese manga and anime series, "Naruto." He is a skilled ninja and one of the central characters in the story. Sasuke initially hails from the prestigious Uchiha clan and has a close relationship with his older brother, Itachi. However, after a tragic incident, Itachi annihilates the Uchiha clan, leading Sasuke on a path of seeking revenge.

Sakura
Sakura Haruno is a fictional character from the Japanese manga and anime series, "Naruto." She is one of the main characters and a kunoichi (female ninja) from the village of Konohagakure. Sakura begins the series as a member of Team 7, alongside Naruto Uzumaki and Sasuke Uchiha, under the guidance of their sensei, Kakashi Hatake.

Kakashi
Kakashi Hatake is a fictional character from the Japanese manga and anime series, "Naruto." He is a highly skilled ninja and serves as the leader of Team 7, mentoring Naruto Uzumaki, Sasuke Uchiha, and Sakura Haruno. Kakashi is known for his calm demeanor, intelligence, and exceptional ninja abilities.
